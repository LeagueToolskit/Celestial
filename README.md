
# ✨ Celestial - League of Legends Custom Skin

Celestial is a custom League of Legends skin project inspired by the beauty of the cosmos. Designed to transform the champion into a celestial entity, this skin features stunning visual effects, cosmic particles, enhanced animations, and a unique aesthetic that delivers an immersive in-game experience.

Whether you're a fan of space-themed cosmetics or simply looking for a fresh way to enjoy your favorite champion, Celestial offers a visually striking alternative while maintaining smooth gameplay and high-quality presentation.

## 🌌 Features

* ⭐ Beautiful celestial-inspired character design
* ✨ Custom visual effects (VFX)
* 🌠 Cosmic particles and animations
* 🎨 High-quality textures and details
* ⚡ Enhanced ability effects
* 🎵 Immersive thematic experience
* 🔧 Compatible with custom skin managers such as CSLoL

## 📥 Installation

1. Download the latest release.
2. Open your preferred custom skin manager.
3. Import the Celestial skin package.
4. Apply the mod and launch the game.
5. Enjoy the celestial experience.
